Let's Breathe

Project Information

Xcode (Swift), application and activity wireframe, Art and Animations

This is a sample Xcode application that helps children with special needs that a breath. This was done in collaberation with Mississpi State University.

I was really into making activity storyboards around this time and I found them useful because it helps visualize a concept and explain it rather than just having the latter part. These storyboards are based on my interpretations of what my team and client suggested. Our clients also suggested that we had a buddy character since our target audience was children with special needs, so I was happy to provide some concept arts that later went into the final prototype product. When working on animating it, I did it in Adobe Animate.
