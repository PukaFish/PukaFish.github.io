Game Development: Introduction

I enjoy making games because I find it as a great way to communicate a story or an idea in an entertaining way.

I am usually tasked with art so I specialize in a cartoon, 2D style. Doing this I have worked with various art programs like Adobe Photoshop and Animate. I have a long history of doing game UI as well. Thankfully, I am not limited to 2D as I also work with 3D assets using Maya, Blender, and Substance Painter.

In terms of programming, I usually deal with programming matters related to the UI interface or various game loops. I have a large focus on working with dialogue systems because I tend to like story based games. Although not entirely programming related, I also have experience in level design. My motto is to make things pretty, but still functional.
